Pasta

Pasta Recipe:

  1. Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
  2. Drain the pasta and set aside.
  3. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add your favorite toppings such as cheese, pepperoni, vegetables, etc.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.
